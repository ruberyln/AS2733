Welcome To The Trackball

What to do :

How the trackball works. This cube has several colors on each side and to view and perform functions you will need to follow the instructions below

Step 1 : Click once to start cube movement then move mouse
Step 2: Double Click to stop the cube movement
Step 3: Hover around object or click and drag mouse to see other sides and colors of the cube, the more you hover the faster the cube moves.
